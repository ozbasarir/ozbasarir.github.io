Why Dogs Love the Beach

2016-09-14

This is Hazel, my daughter’s dog. This morning I took her to the beach and as she does every time, she started running with excitement.

Now, I love going to the beach -maybe because I grew up along the Mediterranean and I was almost addicted to the coast- but I don’t think I ever showed my happiness as much as Hazel does. She makes that happy puppy face, starts running in every direction sometimes sniffing with her nose in the sand and sometimes looking in the distance, alert as a coast guard. If there are birds around, then it’s definitely play time; by the way birds circle around and dive towards Hazel, it seems they enjoy play just as much.
